The Process

Three steps.
One sitting.

No CAD experience. No software to install. Just open a browser and start building.

01

Enter the room.

Type in wall dimensions and ceiling height. Mark where the doors, windows, and appliances land. Most rooms are set up in under two minutes.

02

Build the layout.

Drag base cabinets, uppers, tall cabinets, corner pieces, islands, and appliances from a full catalog. The floor plan and wall elevation update live as you place each piece. Reposition anything at any time.

03

Print the quote.

One click generates a fully itemized PDF — your company name, your pricing, your legal terms, and dual signature lines. Hand it to your customer before you leave the kitchen.
