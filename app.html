Help & FAQ

How do I create a project?
Click New Project in the top header. Enter a customer name and project type, then add rooms.

How do I add a room?
Click + Add Room in the sidebar. Set each wall length (in inches) and the ceiling height.

How do I set up my company info?
Click the ⚙ Company button in the header. Add your name, address, phone, and logo — this info prints on every PDF you export.

What's the difference between a project and a room?
A project is for one customer/job. Each project can have multiple rooms (Kitchen, Laundry, Bathroom, etc.), each with its own cabinet layout.

How do I add a cabinet?
Select a type and dimensions in the left panel, then click Add Cabinet. It will appear on the selected wall.

How do I edit or delete a cabinet?
Double-click any cabinet on the floor plan to open its edit modal. You can change dimensions, notes, or delete it there.

Why can't I see my upper cabinets?
The Show dropdown in the toolbar controls what's visible. Switch to Wall Only to see upper cabinets, or All Cabinets to see both layers at once.

How do I reposition a cabinet?
Click and drag a cabinet on the floor plan to slide it along its wall. Use the From Left field in the edit modal for precise placement.

What does the Dims button do?
Toggles dimension callout lines on the floor plan showing the width of each cabinet.

What is the Elevation view?
A side-on view of each wall showing how base and upper cabinets stack. Switch to it using the Elevation tab at the top of the canvas area.

How do I switch between walls?
Use the North / East / South / West tabs below the canvas toolbar in Elevation view.

Why are some cabinets missing from the elevation?
The elevation shows cabinets on the selected wall only. Make sure the right wall tab is active.

What does Export PDF produce?
A multi-page PDF with: (1) floor plan + company header, (2) a wall elevation for each wall, (3) a full quote page with line items, totals, signature lines, and terms.

What does Print Quote do?
Opens a browser print dialog with a polished quote document — logo, line items, terms, and signature blocks. Best for printing directly or saving as PDF via your browser.

Why does the PDF show "My Cabinet Planner" instead of my company?
Your company profile isn't set up yet. Click ⚙ Company in the header and fill in your name. You'll be warned before exporting if it's not set.

How does cabinet pricing work?
Each cabinet type has a base price formula. Toggle 💲 Pricing in the toolbar to show prices on the floor plan. The Quote modal shows a full breakdown.

How do I add custom job costs?
Open the Quote modal and use the Additional Costs section to add line items like labor, delivery, or hardware.

Can I change the tax rate?
Yes — set it in the Quote modal. It persists per project.
